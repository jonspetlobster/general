# Camp Menu Plan — April 2026 Roadtrip

**Party:** 4 (2 adults, 2 kids)  
**Focus:** High protein, lower carb  
**Equipment:** Propane griddle, Ninja air fryer, stovetop, microwave, dutch oven, fridge, freezer

---

## Protein Snack Stash
Keep these accessible the entire trip:
- Hard boiled eggs (pre-boil 2 dozen at home)
- String cheese / Babybel
- Beef jerky
- Mixed nuts
- Greek yogurt cups
- Rx Bars or similar (real ingredients)

---

## Freezer Prep (Do at Home)
Freeze these before leaving — thaw in order:
- Meatballs + sauce — 2 batches (use Night 2 and White Rim Night 1)
- Sausage links/patties — freeze flat, thaw as needed
- Ground beef burger patties — flatten into pairs, freeze stacked

---

## Phase 1: Outbound (Apr 4–11)

### Day 1 (Apr 4, Sat) — Big drive day
- **Breakfast:** Home, eat before leaving
- **Lunch:** Packed cooler — deli roll-ups, cheese sticks, jerky, fruit
- **Dinner:** Chipotle bowls or fast casual (high protein, skip the chips)

### Day 2 (Apr 5, Sun) — First real camp
- **Breakfast:** Eggs + sausage on griddle
- **Lunch:** Pre-boiled eggs + cheese + crackers
- **Dinner:** Meatballs + sauce in dutch oven *(make big batch, save leftovers)*

### Day 3 (Apr 6, Mon) — Riding day
- **Breakfast:** Shakshuka in dutch oven *(eggs poached in spiced tomato sauce — easy, impressive, very high protein)*
- **Lunch:** Meatball leftovers + salad + string cheese
- **Dinner:** Air fryer chicken thighs + roasted vegetables

### Day 4 (Apr 7, Tue) — Drive to next camp
- **Breakfast:** Greek yogurt + nuts + berries *(no-cook, eat quick)*
- **Lunch:** Egg salad in a wrap + jerky
- **Dinner:** Sausage + spinach soup in dutch oven *(add white beans for extra protein)*

### Day 5 (Apr 8, Wed) — Riding day
- **Breakfast:** Breakfast burritos — eggs, sausage, cheese, light on tortilla
- **Lunch:** Soup leftovers + salad
- **Dinner:** Smash burgers on griddle + coleslaw *(adults go bunless, kids get buns)*

> **Coleslaw:** Bagged cabbage mix + Greek yogurt dressing. Make a big batch — doubles as a side salad on other nights.

### Day 6 (Apr 9, Thu) — Long drive or rest day
- **Breakfast:** Eggs + sausage + hashbrowns on griddle *(the full spread)*
- **Lunch:** Roll-ups + cheese + fruit
- **Dinner:** 🍕 **Calzone night** in dutch oven *(let the kids build theirs — sausage, ricotta, mozzarella)*

### Day 7 (Apr 10, Fri) — Gooseberry Mesa riding
- **Breakfast:** Protein pancakes + sausage *(add protein powder to batter)*
- **Lunch:** Chicken quesadillas on griddle — fast, kids love them
- **Dinner:** Chicken soup in dutch oven *(buy rotisserie at last grocery store, shred into broth with veg)*

### Day 8 (Apr 11, Sat) — Drive to White Rim staging
- **Breakfast:** Eggs + sausage on griddle — fuel up
- **Lunch:** Road stop or diner
- **Dinner:** Air fryer chicken thighs + salad *(simple, high protein, get to bed early)*

---

## Phase 2: White Rim (Apr 12–14)

Truck support means full kitchen gear is available. But after long days, dinner should be **fast**.

### Day 9 (Apr 12, Sun) — Enter Mineral Bottom → Potato Bottom A
- **Breakfast:** Eggs + sausage on griddle
- **Lunch:** Packed wraps — deli meat, cheese, mustard *(easy to eat on trail)*
- **Dinner:** Meatballs + sauce (thawed, in dutch oven) — fast, filling, no effort

### Day 10 (Apr 13, Mon) — Full ride day → Airport A
- **Breakfast:** Shakshuka in dutch oven *(cook while packing camp)*
- **Lunch:** Packed wraps + jerky + nuts + protein bars
- **Dinner:** Smash burgers on griddle + coleslaw *(fast, crowd-pleasing, everyone's happy)*

### Day 11 (Apr 14, Tue) — Exit Shafer Trail → Moab
- **Breakfast:** Greek yogurt + nuts *(no-cook, break camp fast)*
- **Lunch:** Drive into Moab — restaurant stop, you earned it
- **Dinner:** 🎉 **Moab restaurant night.** Celebratory. Let the kids pick.

---

## Phase 3: Return (Apr 15–18)

Energy is lower. Keep it simpler.

### Day 12 (Apr 15, Wed) — Capitol Reef or Ely
- **Breakfast:** Eggs + sausage on griddle
- **Lunch:** Leftovers or deli grab
- **Dinner:** Sausage + spinach soup — warming, easy, comfort food

### Day 13 (Apr 16, Thu) — Drive day
- **Breakfast:** Greek yogurt or grab-and-go
- **Lunch:** Road snacks
- **Dinner:** Air fryer chicken thighs + whatever veg is left

### Day 14 (Apr 17, Fri) — Last camp or drive home
- **Breakfast:** Fridge clear-out scramble — eggs + any meat/veg left, on the griddle
- **Lunch:** Road snacks
- **Dinner:** Home or motel
