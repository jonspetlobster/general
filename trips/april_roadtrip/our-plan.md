## White Rim (Fixed — Apr 12–14)

**Format: Truck support** — F150 hauls gear and water, bikes ride trail sections at will. Leapfrog style, no pack weight on bikes. Much more relaxed than a full bike-pack.

| Date | Day | Details |
|------|-----|---------|
| Apr 12 (Sun) | Day 1 | Enter Mineral Bottom Road (off Hwy 313). Camp: Potato Bottom A |
| Apr 13 (Mon) | Day 2 | White Rim Trail. Camp: Airport A (~45–50 mi by truck) |
| Apr 14 (Tue) | Day 3 | White Rim Trail. Exit Shafer Trail Road → Moab |

**Entry:** Mineral Bottom Road (turn off Hwy 313, ~18 miles from US-191)  
**Exit:** Shafer Trail Road (steep switchbacks, high clearance 4WD required — F150 is fine, take it slow)  
**Fill all water in Moab before staging.** No water at Mineral Bottom or on trail.  
**Fill gas in Moab.** No gas on Hwy 313 or at trail entry.

> ✅ **Permit verified Mar 4, 2026** — details confirmed OK on Recreation.gov.

**Highlights:**
- Capitol Reef — canyon walls, Fremont petroglyphs, fruit orchards
- Bryce Canyon rim (optional 30-min detour off UT-12 on Day 2)
- Zion views from St. George area
- Mojave Desert night sky

## Return: Option 2 — US-50 + Eastern Sierra
*Take this if outbound was Route B (Desert Parks) — see what you missed*

<div style="margin:1rem 0;"><a href="https://www.google.com/maps/dir/Moab,UT/Ely,NV/Lone+Pine,CA/Santa+Cruz,CA" target="_blank" rel="noopener" style="display:inline-block;background:#4285f4;color:white;padding:0.5rem 1.2rem;border-radius:6px;text-decoration:none;font-weight:600;font-size:0.95rem;">🗺️ Open in Google Maps</a></div>

| Date | Day | Drive | Destination | Sleep |
|------|-----|-------|-------------|-------|
| Apr 15 (Wed) | Day 1 | 350 mi | Shafer exit → Ely, NV (via US-50) | Hotel Nevada, Ely (~$70, historic, fun) |
| Apr 16 (Thu) | Day 2 | 230 mi | Ely → Lone Pine, CA (via US-93 → US-395) | BLM dispersed — Alabama Hills (Movie Road) |
| Apr 17 (Fri) | Day 3 | 340 mi | Lone Pine → Santa Cruz | 🏠 Home — a day early |

**Home Apr 17 instead of Apr 18** — this option gets you back a full day early.

**Ely stop:** Hotel Nevada is a legit historic casino hotel, cheap, clean, fun bar. After 3 days on White Rim it'll feel like the Ritz.

**Lone Pine stop:** Alabama Hills at sunset or sunrise is worth it. Hot springs nearby — Wild Willy's (free, off Benton Crossing Rd, 20 min north) if you want to soak before the drive home. Whitney Portal Road for a morning walk.

**Gas warnings:**
- Moab → fill before leaving. Next: Green River (~52 mi on US-191/I-70)
- Ely → Delta UT: 162 miles, only 2 gas stations en route. Fill in Ely.
- Wells/Elko area → Ely via US-93: ~130 miles, limited services. Fill at Elko.
